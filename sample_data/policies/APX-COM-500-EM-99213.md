## Purpose

This policy defines coverage logic for routine professional outpatient evaluation and management services billed under the Apex Health Plan Commercial PPO 500 product.

## Covered Services

Routine outpatient office visits billed with CPT 99212, 99213, 99214, or 99215 are eligible for automated adjudication when all of the following are present:
- the claim is billed on a professional claim format
- place of service is 11
- the servicing provider is active and in network for the member product
- the billed diagnosis supports a routine outpatient evaluation and management encounter
- the billed amount is within configured product guardrails

## Clinical Support Logic

For straight-through approval, the claim should include at least one diagnosis supporting evaluation and management of chronic or stable outpatient conditions. Example support patterns include:
- E11.9 for type 2 diabetes mellitus without complications
- I10 for essential hypertension
- E78.5 for hyperlipidemia

When CPT 99213 is billed with a supported diagnosis and place of service 11, the service is considered appropriate for standard outpatient office evaluation and management under Commercial PPO 500 unless another integrity or utilization rule prevents approval.

## Non-Covered or Review Conditions

The claim should be routed to review when any of the following conditions are present:
- place of service is missing or not equal to 11
- no diagnosis code supports outpatient evaluation and management
- documentation suggests a procedure-intensive visit inconsistent with CPT 99213
- billed charge materially exceeds configured office-visit guardrails for the product

## Adjudication Recommendation

Approve when CPT 99213 is billed with place of service 11 and a covered diagnosis pairing is present.
