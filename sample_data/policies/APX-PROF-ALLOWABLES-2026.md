# Apex Professional Allowables and Pricing Policy 2026

Policy ID: APX-PROF-ALLOWABLES-2026  
Payer: Apex Health Plan  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Review Date: 2026-03-01  
Purpose: Explain mock allowed-amount behavior for professional claims in ClaimsOS demo flows.

## Pricing Intent

ClaimsOS projects a payer allowed amount separate from billed charges so adjudication can show:
- billed amount
- allowed amount
- adjustment amount
- line-level financial context

## Operational Guidance

- In-network office professional claims may be approved with an allowed amount below billed charges.
- Pricing adjustment alone should not imply denial.
- Out-of-network or higher-risk pricing cases may require manual reimbursement review.

## UI / Review Guidance

- Show line-level billed vs allowed values when possible.
- Surface the total adjustment clearly.
- Keep pricing context separate from clinical necessity and referral/auth review outcomes.
