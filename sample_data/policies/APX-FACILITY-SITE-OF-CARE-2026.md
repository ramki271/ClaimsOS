# Apex Facility Affiliation and Site-of-Care Policy 2026

Policy ID: APX-FACILITY-SITE-OF-CARE-2026  
Payer: Apex Health Plan  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Review Date: 2026-03-01  
Purpose: Validate provider/facility alignment for billed professional services.

## Policy Rule

When a claim includes a named facility, the rendering provider should have a documented affiliation with that facility for the billed site-of-care workflow.

## Review Triggers

- Claim facility is not in the provider affiliation list
- Provider affiliation list is not configured
- Surgery or specialty facility claim is billed without matching facility context

## Adjudication Guidance

- Matching facility affiliation supports adjudication confidence.
- Missing or mismatched affiliation should route to review.
- Facility mismatch should be visible in claim detail so reviewers can compare the billed facility against configured affiliations.
