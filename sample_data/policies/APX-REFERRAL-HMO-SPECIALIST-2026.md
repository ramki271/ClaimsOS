# Apex Referral Management Policy: Specialist Access 2026

Policy ID: APX-REF-HMO-SPECIALIST-2026  
Payer: Apex Health Plan  
Product: Commercial HMO Select  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Review Date: 2026-03-01  
Purpose: Define referral requirements for specialist professional claims.

## Summary

Apex HMO specialist services require an active PCP referral when the billed professional service is outside primary care and the member is enrolled in an HMO product.

## Referral Requirement

- Specialist professional claims billed under Apex HMO Select require a referral identifier on the claim record.
- The referral must correspond to the rendering specialist workflow.
- Claims without a referral identifier should route to manual review.

## Example Service Profiles

- Consultation / specialist evaluation codes such as `99244` and `99245`
- Orthopedic specialist services when the member is enrolled in HMO Select
- Cardiology specialist services under HMO referral products

## Adjudication Guidance

- If referral is present and specialist/provider context is valid: continue normal adjudication.
- If referral is absent: route to review, do not straight-through approve.
- If provider is not configured to accept referrals: route to provider review.
